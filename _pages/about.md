Hello! I am a post-master student at the Computer Science at the Yonsei University.
To begin with, I am interested in designing methods that extract rich but explainable knowledge from natural or formal language. In other words, I would like to explore how models learn and generalize essential features when solving specific tasks. Based on this expertise, I am particularly interested in utilizing them for language generation. More specifically, I aim to efficiently generate meaningful data through a specific and targeted approach using the extracted features.


For more information check out my [Publications](https://sungmin630.github.io/publications/) and [CV](https://sungmin630.github.io/cv/).
